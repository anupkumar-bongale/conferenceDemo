About ICAREMA 2026

The International Conference on AI and Robots in Engineering, Medical and Agricultural Applications (ICAREMA 2026) is a premier platform for the convergence of researchers, scientists, and professionals to present their innovative findings and ideas.

The conference aims to explore the integration of Artificial Intelligence and Robotics across a wide range of industries, with a special focus on Engineering systems, Medical technologies, and Agricultural practices. With the growing need for interdisciplinary solutions, ICAREMA 2026 fosters collaboration between academic researchers and industry leaders.

ICAREMA 2026 will be held in hybrid mode — allowing both physical and virtual participation — from April 10 to 12, 2026, in the vibrant and scenic city of Goa, India.

Scope of the Conference

  • AI and Machine Learning algorithms in practical systems
  • Robotics in industrial and medical environments
  • Precision agriculture using AI-powered tools
  • Healthcare automation and diagnostics using AI
  • Robotics for sustainable farming
  • IoT and sensor integration in intelligent systems

Conference Highlights

  • Keynotes by renowned experts from academia and industry
  • Workshops and Tutorials on cutting-edge topics
  • Special Tracks and Poster Sessions
  • Best Paper and Presentation Awards
  • Springer proceedings publication
