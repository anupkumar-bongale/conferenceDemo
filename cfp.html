Call for Papers

The International Conference on AI and Robots in Engineering, Medical and Agricultural Applications (ICAREMA 2026) invites original and high-quality research papers from academia, industry, and government sectors.

Topics of Interest

  • AI in Engineering Systems
  • Robotic Surgery and Medical Devices
  • AI in Precision Agriculture
  • Deep Learning for Medical Imaging
  • Autonomous Agricultural Robots
  • Smart Sensors and IoT Integration
  • Edge AI and Robotics
  • Human-Robot Interaction
  • AI in Bioinformatics and Healthcare
  • Environmental Robotics and Sustainability
  • Swarm Robotics in Agriculture
  • Data Analytics and Predictive Models

Submission Guidelines

All submissions must be original and not under consideration elsewhere. Accepted papers will be published in the conference proceedings and submitted for indexing in major databases.

Papers must be formatted according to the Springer LNNS template and submitted via the EasyChair link (to be provided).
