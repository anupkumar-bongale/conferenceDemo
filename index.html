ICAREMA 2026

International Conference on AI and Robots in Engineering, Medical and Agricultural Applications

April 10–12, 2026 | Goa, India (Hybrid Mode)

About the Conference

ICAREMA 2026 aims to bring together leading academicians, researchers, and industry professionals to exchange and share their experiences and research results on all aspects of Artificial Intelligence and Robotics in Engineering, Medical, and Agricultural domains. The conference will feature keynote addresses, technical paper presentations, workshops, and panel discussions.
