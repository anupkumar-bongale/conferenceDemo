Paper Submission

Authors are invited to submit original research papers that have not been previously published or submitted elsewhere. All accepted and presented papers will be published in the Springer LNNS series.

Submission Instructions

  • Manuscripts must be written in English and follow the Springer LNNS format.
  • Regular papers should not exceed 12 pages (including references).
  • All submissions must be in PDF format.
  • Double-blind review: Author names should be removed from the submitted version.
